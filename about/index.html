About Me

I am a TypeScript full-stack developer proficient in HTML, CSS, React, Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Ant Design, and Formik for front-end development. On the back end, I specialize in Node.js and have a strong command of NestJS. I have a track record of developing websites using TypeScript and related libraries, showcasing my ability to create efficient, type-safe, and visually appealing web applications.

Exploring My Hobbies

Hobbies are an essential part of our lives that allow us to unwind, express ourselves, and learn new skills. In this markdown file, I’ll share some of my favorite hobbies, from energetic activities to creative pursuits.

Rugby - The Thrill of the Game

Rugby is not just a sport; it's a passion. The adrenaline rush of running on the field, strategizing with the team, and the camaraderie among players make rugby an exhilarating experience. Tackling opponents, passing the ball, and scoring tries create moments of excitement that are hard to replicate elsewhere.

Zumba - Dancing to the Rhythm

Zumba is my go-to activity for a lively and enjoyable workout. Dancing to energetic beats while breaking a sweat is not only a fantastic cardiovascular exercise but also a great way to lift spirits. Zumba sessions offer a unique blend of fitness and dance, making it an excellent way to stay active while having a blast.

Kpop - Grooving to the Music

Immersing myself in the world of Kpop is a delightful escape. The catchy tunes, mesmerizing choreography, and captivating music videos make Kpop an irresistible hobby. Whether it’s learning the latest dance moves or singing along to my favorite songs, Kpop brings joy and entertainment into my life.

Singing - Unleashing My Voice

Singing is a form of self-expression that allows me to convey emotions and tell stories through melodies. Whether I’m belting out powerful ballads or humming soothing tunes, singing is a therapeutic hobby that lets me connect with my emotions and share my voice with the world.

Cooking - Culinary Creativity

Exploring new recipes and experimenting with flavors in the kitchen is a passion of mine. Cooking is not just about nourishment; it’s an art form that lets me express my creativity. From whipping up comforting classics to attempting exotic dishes, cooking is a journey of flavors and techniques.

Learning New Languages - Embracing Diversity

Languages are the key to understanding different cultures and connecting with people from around the world. Learning a new language opens doors to new experiences, broadens my perspective, and enables meaningful interactions with individuals from diverse backgrounds.

Bakery - Creating Sweet Delights

Baking is a delightful blend of science and art. Mixing ingredients, watching dough rise, and savoring the aroma of freshly baked goods bring immense satisfaction. From crafting intricate pastries to whipping up simple cookies, baking allows me to create edible masterpieces that delight both the eyes and the taste buds.

Conclusion

My hobbies encompass a wide range of activities, from physical pursuits that keep me active and energized to creative endeavors that allow me to express myself and explore new horizons. Engaging in these hobbies brings joy, balance, and fulfillment to my life, making each day a unique and enjoyable journey. Whether it’s the rush of the rugby field, the rhythm of Zumba, the melodies of singing, or the culinary creations from baking, my hobbies enrich my life and remind me of the importance of embracing the things that bring me happiness.
